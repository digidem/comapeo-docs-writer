## About this Guide

This site provides an introduction to CoMapeo and training resources for both CoMapeo and related tools and applications. 

The site is divided into the following sections:

**Preparing to use CoMapeo**
This section opens with an overview to help clarify what to expect when using CoMapeo. The [Getting Started Essentials](https://lab.digital-democracy.org/comapeo-docs/docs/category/getting-started---essentials) includes practical recommendations and instructions for gearing up to use CoMapeo, based on common technical accompaniment inquiries and training modules. 

And for teams needing a more customized experience of CoMapeo, there are detailed guides, that are essential for your technical support team, for creating custom category sets and bespoke offline maps. 

**Collected Data**
This section provides overviews and walkthroughs of all features related to gathering, reviewing and editing the GIS information, data and other media that can be collected with CoMapeo.  

**Data Privacy & Security**
This section details how data privacy and security considerations are addressed in CoMapeo‚Äôs code base and features, to keep people and data safe.

**Mapping with Collaborators**
This section is aimed at people working within a team, using CoMapeo for a joint project. . It covers the related topics: Projects and Exchange.  There are a number of resources and walkthroughs included for each topic, aimed at supporting teams to work together successfully. 

**Sharing Observations Outside of CoMapeo**
This section provides detailed steps about how to share collected data outside CoMapeo, either with other individuals or organizations, or for use within other applications. This is recommended for decision makers and for the technical  leads in your team.

**Miscellaneous /Appendix**
FAQ, Glossary, Troubleshooting Guide and other companion resources for CoMapeo use.
